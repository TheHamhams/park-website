Welcome to Reality Park!


Park Founder Gruff Hornswaggle

The Glorious Founder

Back in 1979, park founder Gruff Hornswaggle found himself disgusted at the park system. He felt that park had been coddling our children for too long. Gruff made it his mission to create a park that would both physically and mentally challenge children of Florida to prepare them for the future.

Popular Attractions!

Opportunity Yard

Children have had too much given to them on a silver platter. At Opportunity Yard, the only things given to park goers are memories, souveniers, and hopefully a tetnus shot beforehand.

Opportunity Yard allows children to find and create their own toys using their own grit, tools, and imagination. With new junk brought in weekly, every visit is a new experience.

The Greenhouse

Reality Park prides itself on the hands off and natural methods they use to allow native plants to grow in The Greenhouse. If the next generation only learns about "desirable" plants like roses and tulips, they will never discover the native beauty in their own back yards. The Greenhouse allows children the freedom and access to touch, smell, and taste* nature.

* Reality park is not legally responsible for any injury or illness resulting from children tasting or taking part in any attractions. Please visit our waivers page for more information.

Rock River

What kid doesn't like rocks? At Rock River, the park has rocks of all shapes and sizes. Pet rocks have made some people millionairs. Could your child find the inspiration to do the same here?
